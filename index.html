About Us

Imagine getting across Riyadh in just 10 minutes — no traffic, no noise, no pollution. At Samaardh Aviation, we’re turning that vision into a reality.

Samaardh Aviation is an emerging urban air mobility startup based in Saudi Arabia, focused on developing the Kingdom’s first electric air taxi platform using eVTOL (Electric Vertical Take-Off and Landing) technology.

We are building a full-stack solution that integrates modern eVTOL aircraft, app-based on-demand booking, and strategically located vertiports across key urban zones like airports, hospitals, and business centers. Our model is designed to support daily commutes, premium tourism, and urgent logistics for both consumers and enterprises.

Our Services

  • Skip traffic with our electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft, designed for short, efficient urban flights. Reach your destination across the city in under 10 minutes.

  • Easily book flights through our intuitive mobile app with real-time route customization, dynamic pricing, and instant confirmation — just like calling a ride.

  • Take off and land from conveniently located vertiports across Riyadh (and soon other cities), including airports, business hubs, and tourism destinations.

  • Our aircraft are fully electric and designed for low-noise operations, offering a cleaner, quieter alternative to traditional transport.

  • Enjoy a high-end travel experience tailored for professionals, tourists, and time-sensitive commuters — no crowds, no delays, just seamless flying.

Our Technology

  • Electric Propulsion
    Our eVTOL aircraft are 100% electric, producing zero emissions while delivering high thrust-to-weight efficiency for smooth vertical take-off and landing.
  • Noise-Optimized Rotor Design
    We use advanced rotor configurations and noise-reduction technologies to ensure near-silent operation — ideal for urban environments.
  • Smart Navigation Algorithms
    Our system leverages real-time data and AI-powered routing to dynamically optimize flight paths, reduce congestion, and enhance safety.
  • Integrated Flight & Ground Systems
    Our aircraft communicate seamlessly with vertiports and the booking app to provide a fully integrated, automated, and reliable experience.

Aircraft Models

Model A: Sky-Rider One

Sky-Rider One is our next-generation air mobility solution built to revolutionize personal and shared transportation in dense urban areas and city-to-city routes. Combining comfort, safety, and intelligent automation, it delivers an elevated travel experience.

Design Specifications

  • Configuration: 6-rotor eVTOL platform
  • Capacity: 4 passengers + luggage
  • Structure: Lightweight carbon fiber composite
  • Autonomy: Semi-autonomous with AI-assisted navigation
  • Cabin: Panoramic glass windows with noise-cancelling interiors

Features

  • AI-assisted autonomous flight systems
  • Quiet rotor design for urban compatibility
  • Fast charging and quick turnaround operations

Model B: Cargo Falcon

Cargo Falcon is our high-capacity, autonomous cargo aircraft built to handle heavy payloads and last-mile delivery challenges in urban, rural, and remote environments. It will be mainly used for Industrial Logistics, Food & Medical Deliveries and Smart Supply Chains.

Design Specifications

  • Configuration: Tilt-wing quad-rotor with fixed cargo bay
  • Payload Capacity: 500–800 kg
  • Cargo Volume: 1.8 m³
  • Loading Mechanism: Rear hatch and modular drone-docking pallets
  • Propulsion: Fully electric

Features

  • VTOL/STOL support for compact or unpaved landing areas
  • Autonomous flight with real-time adaptive routing
  • Roll-on/roll-off cargo system for time-sensitive payloads

Model C: Rescue Wing

Rescue Wing is our versatile, mission-critical aircraft designed for high-speed emergency deployments. From emergency evacuations to Hajj & Umrah medical response, it is built for reliability under pressure.

Design Specifications

  • Configuration: Tilt-rotor hybrid wing
  • Capacity: 1 medical technician, 1 patient (stretcher-compatible)
  • Cabin: Pressurized medical-grade cabin with essential life support systems

Features

  • Night vision and FLIR (forward-looking infrared) cameras
  • Integrated winch system for extraction in non-landing zones
  • Real-time telemetry for coordination with ground teams
